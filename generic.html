About Me !

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I am Sriram Thatipamula, a dedicated Data Science graduate with a passion for analyzing complex datasets and deriving valuable insights to drive data-driven decision-making. With 11 months of hands-on experience as a Data Analyst at Cognizant Technology Solutions in Hyderabad, India, I have honed my skills in data collection, cleansing, preprocessing, and visualization, utilizing tools and technologies such as Python, SQL, Tableau, and Excel.

During my Master's journey at the University of Memphis, I delved deep into courses ranging from Data Science and Machine Learning to Data Analytics, Technical Analysis, and Statistical Methods. My academic projects have allowed me to explore and evaluate various machine learning algorithms, including KNN, Support Vector Machines, Decision Trees, and Neural Networks, for tasks such as accent recognition using Mel Frequency Cepstral Coefficients (MFCC).

In addition to my technical expertise, I pride myself on my excellent communication, team collaboration, critical thinking, problem-solving, and customer support skills. These soft skills have been instrumental in my ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, communicate insights to stakeholders, and identify data-driven solutions to business challenges.

Certified in Python programming and Technical English for Engineers, I am committed to continuous learning and professional development. I am eager to leverage my diverse skill set and expertise as a Data Analyst, Data Scientist, Machine Learning Engineer, Business Analyst, or Software Engineer to contribute to the growth and success of forward-thinking organizations.

Whether you're interested in exploring my academic projects, professional experience, or simply learning more about my journey in the world of data science and analytics, I invite you to navigate through my portfolio and connect with me on LinkedIn or via email. Let's innovate, collaborate, and make data work for us!
