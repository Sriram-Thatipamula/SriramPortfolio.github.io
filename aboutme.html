About Me !

Welcome to my professional portfolio! I’m Sriram, a passionate data science graduate dedicated to uncovering the stories hidden within data. My journey into the world of data science began with a curiosity about how data can drive decision-making and create impactful solutions. Over the years, I have refined my skills across various domains, including data analysis, data engineering, machine learning, and data visualization. Each of these areas has allowed me to explore innovative approaches to problem-solving and to contribute meaningfully to data-driven projects.

I hold a Master’s degree in Data Science from the University of Memphis, where I established a strong foundation in database management systems (DBMS), data analysis, big data technologies, data visualization, machine learning, statistical and biostatistical analysis, data mining, and cloud computing platforms. My academic journey ignited a deep fascination with extracting insights from complex datasets, prompting me to explore a variety of tools and technologies, including Python, R, SQL, Tableau, and more. I am committed to continually enhancing my knowledge and staying updated on the latest trends and advancements in the field of data science.

Professionally, I have worked with leading organizations, including Cognizant Technology Solutions as a Programmer Analyst (Data Science Focused) and Inteq Software LLP as a Data Engineer and Data Analyst Intern. During my tenure, I contributed to various data science projects, gaining experience in handling diverse datasets, optimizing workflows, and collaborating with teams to deliver data-driven solutions.

In addition to my technical expertise, I pride myself on my excellent communication, team collaboration, critical thinking, problem-solving, and customer support skills. These soft skills have been instrumental in my ability to collaborate effectively with cross-functional teams, communicate insights to stakeholders, and identify data-driven solutions to business challenges.

I am committed to continuous learning and professional development. I am eager to leverage my diverse skill set and expertise as a Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Data Engineer, Data Scientist or Machine Learning Engineer to contribute to the growth and success of forward-thinking organizations.

Whether you're interested in exploring my academic projects, professional experience, or simply learning more about my journey in the world of Data Science, I invite you to navigate through my portfolio and connect with me on LinkedIn or via email. Let's innovate, collaborate, and make data work for us!
